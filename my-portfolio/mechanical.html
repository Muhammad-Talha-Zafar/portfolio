Mechanical Design & Kinematics

From conceptual sketching and CAD modeling to structural analysis and final physical assembly. I focus on precision tolerances, material optimization, and high-torque actuation systems.

PROTOTYPE V1.2

01. Autonomous Mobile Base (AMR)

A 4-wheeled differential drive system designed for indoor navigation. Focus was placed on chassis rigidity and vibration dampening for sensitive LIDAR sensors.

Technical Specifications

Drive SystemDifferential Drive / Omni-wheels
Chassis MaterialAluminum 6061-T6 / Acrylic Pannels
Payload Capacity15kg (Tested)
Ground Clearance45mm

Minor Engineering Details

  • Tolerances: Machined motor mounts to +/- 0.05mm precision.
  • Fasteners: Utilized M3 socket head cap screws with Loctite 242 for vibration resistance.
  • Weight Dist: Battery placed at geometric center to optimize center of mass for high-speed turns.

02. 6-DOF Industrial Manipulator

An articulated robotic arm designed for pick-and-place automation. This project involved complex linkage design and inverse kinematics calculations.

Actuation & Torque

Joint 1-3High-Torque Planetary Gear Steppers
Reduction Ratio50:1 Cycloidal Gearbox
Reach Radius650mm
End EffectorInterchangeable Vacuum/Servo Gripper

Structural Analysis

  • FEA Testing: Conducted stress analysis on elbow joint to ensure Safety Factor of 2.5.
  • Bearings: Dual-row ball bearings used at base to handle high axial and radial loads.
  • Wire Mgmt: Internal cable routing channels integrated into the 3D-printed arm segments.

03. Bio-Inspired Legged System

A quadruped robot focusing on dynamic stability and leg linkage optimization. Modeled extensively in SolidWorks before fabrication.

Fabrication Details

Leg MaterialCarbon Fiber Infused Nylon (PA12-CF)
Joint TypeDirect Drive Actuators
Total Weight4.2kg
Shock AbsorbIntegrated TPU Spring Dampeners

Mechanism Logic

  • Hinges: Needle roller bearings used for minimal friction in high-frequency gait cycles.
  • Sensing: Hall-effect encoders integrated directly into joint housings for real-time feedback.
  • Heat Sink: Custom aluminum plates for motor drivers to dissipate heat during peak load.
